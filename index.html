questions

How long until I see calls coming in?

Foundation tier ships your site in week one and begins ad management in week two. Most clients see their first booked appointments inside the first 30 days. Pro tier typically delivers measurable lead flow within the first 14 days because the Content Engine + AI Front Office go live in parallel with paid acquisition.

How are you different from a regular marketing agency?

We’re not an agency, we’re a fractional growth team. Agencies sell hours of work; we sell booked appointments. Pricing reflects that — you pay one flat monthly number, not a $1,500–$10,000 retainer plus per-hour billing. We also bundle the toolchain you’d otherwise stitch together from 12–14 vendors. One throat to choke when something breaks.

What happens if I want to leave?

You take everything with you. Domain stays in your name. Site exports as a ZIP, one click. Google Workspace stays yours. Tracked phone numbers port out. Month-to-month, no contracts. We just don’t want you to leave — that’s the deal.

Do I need an existing website?

No. The setup fee covers building you a new site from scratch — copywriting, design, technical setup, hosting, SSL. If you have an existing domain you want to bring, perfect. If not, we register one for you (~$15/yr at any registrar).

What's the marketing budget I should plan for?

Foundation tier works at any ad budget; we’ll match strategy to spend. For Pro tier we recommend $4,000/mo minimum in ad budget for the math to work consistently — below that the AI Front Office and Content Engine carry too much of the load. We have flexible payment options including milestone-tied tranches if cash flow is tight in month one.

Why a discovery call instead of just signing up?

We work with one business per ZIP. The discovery call is how we make sure (a) your territory is open, (b) the math works for your specific business, (c) we’re actually a fit. 20 minutes. No pitch deck, no high-pressure close. If we’re not the right partner we’ll tell you who is.

Who's behind Slatepress?

Anthony DeMeo. Operator. Sold a programmatic local-content business in 2019. Building Slatepress as the AI-native version of that playbook for local service businesses. Direct cell access for Pro-tier clients; you talk to the person who built the system.
